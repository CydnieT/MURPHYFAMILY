Purpose & Scope

This codex is a structured index for the Murphy family’s records and planning materials. It is intended to organize documents for efficient attorney review and long-term archival continuity.

Notice: This is an organizational framework and does not constitute legal advice. A licensed attorney should review and finalize any estate, trust, or corporate filings.

Motto

FORTITUDO · FIDES · IMPERIUM

Strength · Loyalty · Sovereignty

Murphy Made

House mark and commerce records, maintained separately from estate/legal instruments while indexed here for continuity.

Records to Maintain

  • Brand marks, label templates, packaging lockups
  • Batch logs: ingredients, process notes, safety notes
  • Invoices, receipts, banking references

Attorney-Facing Notes

If forming or operating a business entity, confirm licensing, tax registration, product compliance requirements, and liability coverage with appropriate professionals.

The Midnight Garden

Homestead and wellness documentation pillar, organized for resilience and archive continuity.

Records to Maintain

  • Garden plans, rotations, seed inventory
  • Herbal SOPs, recipes, batch records, safety notes
  • Infrastructure logs: water, power, storage, maintenance

Integration

This pillar may feed Murphy Made (product output) and the Family Archive (legacy documentation). Keep consistent naming and versioning.

Assets & Imports

Drop-in structure for PDFs, images, and data exports, optimized for permanence and review.

Recommended Folders

  • /docs/pdfs/ (legal, financial, health)
  • /docs/images/ (crests, seals, scans)
  • /docs/data/ (CSV, JSON, Base64)

Printing / Export
